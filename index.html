Why agents beat one-off website builders

AI website builders like Atoms or Genspark generate impressive starting points, but Super goes further by behaving like a teammate. She can open those tools, review output, push changes to GitHub, redeploy, and repeat — the same way a human would — as supported by reports on modern AI agents and builders from atoms.dev and genspark.ai.

Super’s approach aligns with broader trends toward agentic workflows highlighted in developer communities and open-source agent projects on github.com.
